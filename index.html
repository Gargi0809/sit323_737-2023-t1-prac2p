WELCOME TO OREXI

OAKLEIGH’S FAVOURITE SOUVLAKI SHOP

Oakleigh’s oldest and favorite Souvlaki bar is located at 33 Chester Street, still serves our famous Charcoal grilled gyro and we have now also introduced several more traditional Greek meals like Pasticio and Mousaka. If you’re planning one meal in Oakleigh, it should be the charcoal grilled Orexi souvlaki rolled in warm pita bread, or the one of their generous open plate souvlakia. While you are there make sure you also try our Coffees.

FOOD

Orexi Souvlaki is renowned for the delicious charcoal grilled Souvlaki in warm pita bread as well as their huge open plate souvlaki dishes. We also prepare daily home made meals including: Moussaka, Pasticcio, and Oven baked lemon potatoes

COFFEE

Each morning our shop is frequented by our loyal customers who come in for a Greek coffee, or their favourite coffee.
